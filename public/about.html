About Me

I'm 23 years old, I'm currently working in the food industry, looking to change careers and follow my true passions.

I've always had a passion for Information Technology, and like many others, my interest started with video games. I first became curious about technology when my PSP broke. It gave me the chance to tear it down and understand how it worked. After that, I wanted to learn how the world created these amazing devices. This curiosity followed me into adulthood. I've built multiple computers for myself and others, troubleshot various issues, and even bought broken game systems just to fix them myself.

I consider myself fairly knowledgeable about IT, and for a while, I thought I would work as a technician or network administrator, something in that field. While IT is a fantastic hobby, my true passion lies in programming. I can't quite explain it, but I can't imagine working in anything other than coding. The ability to create anything I want is incredibly fulfilling to me. On top of that, my dream is to become a game developer. :]
